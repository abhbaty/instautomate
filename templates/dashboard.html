⚠️ DM Limitation Notice
Instagram DM Restrictions The Graph API can only send DMs to users who have previously messaged your business, or when your app has the instagram_manage_messages permission approved for DM automation. Apply through Meta's App Review process.

Comment replies are public and do not have this restriction.
